Our Coaches

We have 7 dedicated coaches with diverse experiences in engineering, programming, and robotics. Their guidance helps us learn and grow. However, it’s the students who do the hard work and bring the ideas to life. Our coaches provide invaluable mentorship and insights, but it’s the students’ hard work and dedication that make the real difference.

What is FRC?

The FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an exciting and challenging event where high school students collaborate to design, build, and program robots. Each year, a new game is revealed where teams must overcome various challenges and complete tasks. FRC is not just about robots—it's about learning, teamwork, and making lifelong memories!

FRC Robotics Glossary

Accelerometer

A sensor that measures the acceleration force in multiple axes, often used to detect robot movement or tilt.

Actuator

A device that converts energy into motion, often used to move parts of the robot, such as arms, wheels, or doors.
